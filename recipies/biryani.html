Overview:

Biryani is a fragrant rice dish layered with marinated meat or vegetables, aromatic spices, and herbs. It originated in Persia and was perfected in Indian royal kitchens.

Key Ingredients:

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken, mutton, or vegetables
  • Yogurt, saffron, onions
  • Ginger, garlic
  • Whole spices (cardamom, cloves, cinnamon), mint, and coriander

Preparation Steps:

  1. Marinate meat in yogurt, garlic, ginger, and spices.
  2. Parboil rice separately with whole spices.
  3. In a heavy-bottomed pot, layer the rice and marinated meat.
  4. Cook on low heat (dum method) to infuse flavors and finish cooking.
